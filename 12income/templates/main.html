Why 12Income is good for everyone?

Easy for Everyone

12Income is designed to be light weighted and easy to use. Everything is on the same page. There are no complex configurations, you can simply start doing accounting by typing a number.

Instant Change

Any record item can be switched to income or expense with just one click. With the help of action integrated calendar, you can drag and drop items between day slots. Managing account is so easy! You will love this!

Budget Control

It's always good to plan your finance. In 12Income uou can setup your monthly budget and 12Income will monitor your expenses at real time against given budget. It will warn you in case you are overspending.

Recurring

Every record item can be set as a recurring item. 12Income will save you some time by adding this item automatically each month. Of course, you can always stop this recurring at any time.

Customization

Once you sign up for 12Income, it's ready to use. You don't have to setup anything. However you can still do some basic settings to work better with 12Income, e.g. change the currency symbol.

Comprehensive Reports

Straight forward reporting page will help you understand your financial status in real time. It shows you how much money you spend every month and what kinds of staffs you are spending your money on.
